001 / The Problem

A third of all food
is wasted.

Every year, 1.3 billion tonnes of food ends up in landfills — while 800 million people go hungry. That's not a coincidence. That's a choice.

002 / The Impact

Food waste is a climate emergency.

Rotting food produces methane — a greenhouse gas 80× more potent than CO₂. If food waste were a country, it'd be the world's third-largest emitter.

003 / At Home

It starts in your kitchen.

The average household wastes $1,600 in food every year. Plan your meals. Store smarter. Eat what you buy. Simple changes, massive impact.
